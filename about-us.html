Sunnah Tech Lab (STL) is an innovation hub and collaborative community fostering technological growth in northern Nigeria.

At STL, we're committed to building a robust technology ecosystem, starting from Jos North. Our mission is to empower startups, innovators, creatives, and entrepreneurs through community-centric innovation and peer-based, hands-on learning.

We provide a platform that offers the foundation and resources necessary to transform challenges into solutions, and ideas into scalable products and companies. By nurturing local talent and fostering collaboration, we aim to drive sustainable economic growth and technological advancement in our region.

Join us in our mission to catalyze innovation, foster entrepreneurship, and build a brighter, tech-driven future for northern Nigeria.

From vision to reality: The STL journey

Sunnah Tech Lab was born from a vision to bridge the technology gap in northern Nigeria and empower local talent to drive innovation.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators could come together, learn, and build solutions for local and global challenges. Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of technological advancement in our region, fostering a community of creative minds and future leaders.

Through our hands-on programs, mentorship opportunities, and state-of-the-art facilities, we're not just building products – we're building the future of northern Nigeria's tech ecosystem.

Empowering innovation across the spectrum

At Sunnah Tech Lab, we offer a comprehensive suite of programs and resources to support innovators at every stage of their journey.

Our holistic approach to innovation and entrepreneurship encompasses everything from ideation and prototyping to market launch and scaling. We believe in nurturing not just ideas, but the people behind them, creating a supportive ecosystem that fosters growth, collaboration, and sustainable success.

  • Incubation Programs.

    Our flagship incubation programs provide early-stage startups with the resources, mentorship, and support they need to transform their ideas into viable businesses. From workspace and funding to expert guidance and networking opportunities, we offer a comprehensive launchpad for innovation.

  • Tech Skills Training.

    We offer hands-on, practical training in cutting-edge technologies. Our courses cover a wide range of topics, from web and mobile development to artificial intelligence and data science. By equipping local talent with in-demand skills, we're bridging the tech skills gap and fostering a new generation of tech leaders.

  • Entrepreneurship Workshops.

    Our entrepreneurship workshops go beyond tech, focusing on essential business skills such as market research, business model development, financial planning, and pitching. We empower innovators with the knowledge and tools they need to turn their tech solutions into sustainable businesses.

  • Mentorship Network.

    We connect budding entrepreneurs with experienced industry leaders, successful founders, and subject matter experts. Our mentorship program provides invaluable guidance, helping innovators navigate challenges, refine their strategies, and accelerate their growth.

  • Innovation Challenges.

    We regularly organize hackathons and innovation challenges that focus on solving real-world problems in our local communities. These events not only spark creativity and collaboration but also help identify promising solutions and talents that can be further supported through our other programs.
